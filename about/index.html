About

I grew up in Lake Charles, Louisiana, studied at Stanford, and now work in the tech industry.

I started my career at Palantir, where I focused on healthcare and biotech customers. After that, I worked on the product team at Benchling, where I led Data & Analytics products and managed an acquisition. I left that job in 2024 to start Partly, a platform that made it easy to hire people fractionally and full-time from your existing professional networks. We sold Partly to Luminai and I became head of FDE there.

You can read more of my writing here or see some prototypes of projects here.

I live in the SF Bay area. I like poker, ultimate, basketball, and burritos.
