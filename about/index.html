About

I grew up in Lake Charles, Louisiana, studied at Stanford, and now work in the tech industry.

I started my career at Palantir, where I focused on healthcare and biotech customers. For the last 4.5 years I worked on the product team at Benchling, where I spent time on our Insights, Machine Learning, Assays, Lab Automation, Search, Warehouse, In Vivo, and Studies products.

I left my job in February 2024 to start a company. You can read more of my writing at partly.work and the project itself is called Partly.

I live in San Francisco, California.
